- **01_Introduction**
    
    - Problem statement, motivation, research questions
        
    - Contributions (what you built or discovered)
        
    - Thesis roadmap (1 short paragraph)
        
- **02_Background / Literature Review**
    
    - Prior work and what’s missing
        
    - Concepts needed to understand your work
        
    - Your “gap” and how you address it
        
- **03_Data and Context** (sometimes merged with Methods)
    
    - Data sources, inclusion/exclusion, data dictionary summary
        
    - Ethics, privacy, IRB (if applicable)
        
    - Data quality, missingness, bias, limitations
        
- **04_Methods / Methodology**
    
    - Study design (qual, quant, mixed, or project design)
        
    - Preprocessing, feature engineering, modeling, evaluation
        
    - Interview protocol + thematic analysis approach (if qualitative)
        
    - Tools and implementation details (reproducibility)
        
- **05_Results**
    
    - Main findings (tables/figures)
        
    - Model performance, ablations, error analysis (if ML)
        
    - Themes + illustrative quotes (if qualitative)
        
- **06_Discussion**
    
    - Interpretation: what the results mean and why they matter
        
    - Compare to related work
        
    - Practical implications (for stakeholders)
        
    - Threats to validity / limitations
        
- **07_Conclusion and Future Work**
    
    - Crisp summary of contributions
        
    - What you’d do next with more time/data
        
    - Deployment or adoption considerations (if relevant)
